Presented at the NYC Tech Meetup on the emerging local-first software movement and its practical implications.

## Topics

- What "local-first" means and why it matters for user experience
- CRDTs vs OT — when to use which
- Real-world examples: Figma, Linear, Notion
- Building a local-first todo app with Automerge in 30 minutes (live demo)

## Key Insight

> Cloud software gave us collaboration but took away ownership. Local-first gives us both.

## Audience

120 attendees. The live demo sparked a 40-minute Q&A session — the longest in the meetup's history.
